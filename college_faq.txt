Fee Reimbursement (Telangana Govt.)
Eligible students under EWS, BC, SC, ST categories can apply for fee reimbursement through Telangana State Govt. portal.

Reimbursement covers Convener Quota tuition fees (₹1,35,000 per year).

Students must submit income certificate, caste certificate, EAMCET rank card, and bonafide certificates during admission.
